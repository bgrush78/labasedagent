How your home is positioned and presented makes a real difference in what you walk away with.

Marketing that actually markets

Professional photography, targeted digital advertising, social reach, and a presentation strategy built around your specific buyer. Not a template, a plan designed for your home.

Priced to perform

We'll review comparable sales and current market conditions together so you understand exactly how we landed on your number. Overpricing costs you time and money. We'll get it right from the start.

The selling process, step by step

  1. Pricing conversation

    We'll look at recent comparable sales and active competition in your neighborhood together. You'll understand the reasoning behind the number, not just receive it.

  2. Prepare the home

    I'll walk through with you and tell you honestly what's worth addressing before you go live. Small things can have an outsized impact on how buyers perceive value. I won't waste your time or money on things that don't move the needle.

  3. Build your marketing plan

    This is where my background sets your listing apart. We'll put together a plan that includes professional photography, targeted digital ads, personalized outreach, social content, and MLS placement, all coordinated to create a strong first impression and sustained interest.

  4. Hit the market

    Your home goes live on MLS, Zillow, Redfin, Realtor.com, and across my channels simultaneously. We track interest from day one and adjust if needed.

  5. Review and negotiate offers

    I'll help you evaluate every offer clearly: price, terms, contingencies, and what each one actually means for your timeline and net proceeds. The highest offer isn't always the best one.

  6. Through to closing

    I stay engaged through every step of escrow. Inspections, repair requests, paperwork: I handle it so you don't have to and make sure the finish line actually gets crossed.
